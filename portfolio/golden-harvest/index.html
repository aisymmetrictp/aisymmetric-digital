About Us

Four Generations on This Land

Golden Harvest has been farmed by the Brennan family since 1928. In 2010, third-generation farmer Katie Brennan transitioned the operation to certified organic practices. Today we cultivate 120 acres of vegetables, herbs, and small fruits.
