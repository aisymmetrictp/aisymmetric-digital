Our Story

Dance Is for Everyone

Nova Dance Studio was founded by professional dancer Mia Torres to create an inclusive space where people of all backgrounds can discover the joy of movement. Our faculty includes former Broadway performers, music video choreographers, and nationally certified dance educators.
